Staten Island Solo Artists

RZA

Years Active: 1984-present

Aka: RZArector, Prince Rakeem, The Scientist, Bobby Digital, Bobby Dynamite

RZA (Ruler Zig-Zag-Zig Allah) is the founder and producer for Wu-Tang Clan. His cousins are also members of Wu-Tang: Ol' Dirty Bastard and GZA.

RZA remixed the beat for 7th Chamber, resulting in the following track: 7th Chamber II. Also, his best verse (in my opinion) is on this track and begins at 4:00 in 7th Chamber (2:55 in 7th Chamber II).

GZA

Years Active: 1990-present

Aka: The Genius

GZA (Genius Zig-Zag-Zig Allah) is one of the original 9 members of the Wu-Tang Clan. He is also the cousin of other Wu-Tang members: RZA and ODB.

Ol' Dirty Bastard

Years Active: 1992-2004

Aka: Ason Unique, The Specialist

Ol' Dirty Bastard (ODB) is one of the original 9 members of the Wu-Tang Clan. He is also the cousin of other Wu-Tang members: RZA and GZA, and Wu-Tang affiliate 60 second assassin. In addition to them, he is also cousins with Flavor Flav, a member of Public Enemy from Long Island.

ODB died of a drug overdose in 2004 at the age of 35.
