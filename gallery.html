Counter-Composition XIII (Theo Van Doesburg 1925-26)

Van Doesburg created this Counter-Composition in response to Piet Modrian's insistence upon strictly using vertical or horizontal lines in artwork. This method of Piet's is known as Neo-Plasticism, and the variant that Van Doesburg created was a modification of Neo-Plasticism known as Elementarism. An example of this can be seen in this static painting or this interactive version.
